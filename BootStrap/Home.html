Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan's College of Engineering is an engineering college in Mumbai, India, established in 1990.
The college is a tribute to the late Shri Vasantdada Patil, the Sahakar Maharshi and former Chief Minister of Maharashtra.
